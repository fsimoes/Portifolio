My first year

My first year in St. Lawrence college was whole new experience in my life and not only academic but personal as well since I am an international student. Back in Brazil I used to be game/app developer, I was self-taught and that was hardening my search to improve my career, which is work for a main game company as a gameplay programmer and making a triple A game (games with high development budgets and worldwide promotion).

After I settle in this new city, Cornwall, I began to focus on my studies, since I had previous knowledge of gaming programming I decided to improve every class project, I wanted to not only do a homework, It would be my portfolio as well.

After a few classes I could analyze and determine what would be my biggest challenges, in the end, It came to.

  • C++
  • Math
  • Comm
  • All of the above, in English

On my previous experience I used more high level programming languages, such as ActionScript, C# or javascript, but on the first day of class I was introduced to C++, this was a lower language level, not much but lower than what I was used. And on top of that, usually, just the name of Math and Comm are enough to send a chill down to anyone spine, but now in English, which is not my main language, I had to work hard on this.

And in the end of the first year I manage to score a GPA of 4.0, meaning that I had A+ in all my subjects so far and create 3 games with decent art and gameplay, one of them is sonic themed, the other one is Zelda themed and the last and more complete of them it is a reimagination of the classic game Frogger, this last one was converted into a platform game and became the cover of the Game Programming Course flyer, which is my precious achievement of this first year.

During the summer, I decided to create my own game, so I came with a some ideas and put them together, but I was overwhelmed with my full-time job and had to stop and decided to create it during my lunch break during college, so far I manage to start it and it is going well, I want to finish it before week 10, because this term, It will be way more harder, the professors announced several final projects and by December we are going to handle in 5 projects, all with different approaches, technologies and mostly in 3D.

I am really excited about this last year, I can’t wait to see all my projects done, because after all, creating a nice resume and a full portfolio is the only mean that I might be able to achieve my goal.

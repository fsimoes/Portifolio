Ludz

To learn about the history of his ancestors, the young Ludz must reach the distant archipelago of Insulam. There, energy levels are measured according to the accumulated knowledge and each island corresponds to a distinct activity.

In this game, which combines adventure and learning, elementary and high school students can learn, in a playful way, how to develop concepts of Portuguese and mathematics.

In addition, as students progress through the phase, simultaneous reports are sent to teachers.

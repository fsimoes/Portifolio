Game of Churros

You asked the vendor for a churros, but the vendor is crazy! Now you have to collect as much churros as you can. How hungry are you? Compete worldwide, and challenge your friends!

This was my first game fully developed, I applied it for Intel's GameDev contest in Rio de Janeiro, reaching the 2nd place, later distributed through the FGL, reaching 48 sites and 5000+ players from more than 70 countries.
